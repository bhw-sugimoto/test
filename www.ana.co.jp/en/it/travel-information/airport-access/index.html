Access search for departure/arrival

You can now get information on how to get to the departure airport and board your flight, and also on how to get to your final destination after disembarkation.

Service Overview

This service offers a seamless travel experience by integrating up-to-date flight status information and location information such as the terminal, boarding gate and security checkpoint with transit planner search services to provide you with comprehensive route information for your entire journey. This covers getting to the departure airport and onto your flight, as well as traveling onward to your final destination after disembarkation. Thanks to our partnerships with ground transportation providers*1, you can also reserve and purchase tickets for getting to your departure airport*2.

  • * Airport access searches are not available for airports outside Japan.
  • *1.
    We are gradually expanding our partnerships with ground transportation providers.
  • *2.
    Some products can be reserved/purchased in Travel CUBE, and some must be reserved on the ground transportation provider’s website.

How to Use This Service

After booking your ANA ticket, go to the official ANA app home screen or the Reservation Details screen on the ANA website, and then go to the Airport Access page. Enter your start point and destination, and search how to get to the airport.

  • * All images are for illustrative purposes only.

Using the Service on the ANA Website

STEP 1 Login Screen

Log in via the View Reservation page on the ANA website.

STEP 2 Flight Ticket Reservation Details Screen

Click the Airport Access Search link.

STEP 3 Airport Access Search conditions selection screen

Enter your start point and time of arrival at the airport, and click Search.

STEP 4 Airport Access Search results screen

On this screen, you can confirm information for your flight such as how to get to the airport, the terminal, and the boarding gate.

  • * For further details, please see Airport Access Search results screen.

STEP 1 Official ANA App

Select Airport Access from the official ANA app home screen.

Enter your destination and click Search.

On this screen, you can confirm your flight's scheduled departure time and route information all the way up to your destination.

Airport Access Search Results Screen

As well as information on how to travel to/from the airport in keeping with the scheduled departure/arrival time of your reserved flight, you can also confirm information such as the terminal, security checkpoint, airport map, boarding gate number, and flight status.

  • * The information available will differ depending on your flight. Please also note that this information will only be available during certain times.

Tokyo Subway Ticket (Tokyo Metro / Toei Subway lines)

Available to purchase through Airport Access, the Tokyo Subway Ticket is a cheap and convenient special passenger ticket which enables tourists to enjoy unlimited travel on a total of 13 lines, namely all 9 Tokyo Metro lines and all 4 Toei Subway lines. When using Airport Access, you can purchase the Common One-day Ticket for Tokyo Metro & Toei Subway (which is sold at Tokyo Metro and Toei Subway stations) for an even cheaper price. You can easily pick up your Tokyo Subway Ticket by visiting one of the main Tokyo Metro / Toei Subway stations and scanning your QR code (which you will receive after purchasing your ticket) against the QR code reader on the ticket machine for tourists. You can do this at any time within the relevant station's operating hours.

  • * The ticket is valid for 24 hours, 48 hours, or 72 hours from the time you first insert it into the slot at a set of automatic ticket gates. The applicable validity period can be confirmed on the reverse side of your ticket.
  • * For information on the stations at which you can board and disembark trains, please see the map for the Tokyo Metro and Toei Subway lines. This ticket cannot be used for travel on any of the JR lines.
